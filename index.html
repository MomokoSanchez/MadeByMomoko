Clean Code

I believe in simplicity. No spaghetti code and no overly special complex scripts that might break your design at this one certain resolution. Instead of reinventing the wheel, I try to find solutions, that have been well-tried to combine them to the optimal performance.

BRAGI

Bragi is a leading innovater in hearable computing and brought the first wireless smart earphone to the market, long before Apple decided to ditch the headphone jack.
Being a fast moving company, they constantly need presentation of their products - The Dash, The Headphone and now The Dash Pro. To mirror their innovative approach, their marketing pages also needed to be modern and innovative.
The goal was to implement pages for the new products and OS based on the designer's template as well as to migrate existing pages from multiple platforms to Wordpress.

MADE WITH

The challenge was to build marketing pages that would run smooth with custom animations and background videos. Since there were multiple developers over time, it was necessary to create a workflow that would merge the developers contributions on local environments that needed to be synchronized with testing and live environments. Parts of the existing websites were created on different systems and partly without backend, so they needed to be unified to one CMS to ensure optimal management.

RESULTS

The pages are built on a boostrap template and most of the animations are implemented with scrollmagic. The develoment environment was set up with Vagrant and synchronized with Github. To improve the performance, the content is delivered by a CDN from Amazon Web Services and Google Analytics was implemented to measure user interaction on the Marketing Pages as well as on the shop.
The migration of the sites to Wordpress required the CMS to have a customized template, work with a CDN and being able add language options which was achieved by multiple plugins and custom solutions.

VERTEMO

Vertemo is a event technology website that serves mostly as a business card. The original page was outdated, so a complete redesign was necessary.

MADE WITH

Since the page has mainly representative purpose, it needed to be simple and clear, so the design was based on the one page template Ageny[link]. There weren't many content changes expected to be and the web space was limited, for this reason the page is pure frontend without a CMS.

RESULTS

The template is based on Boostrap 3 and the scrolling animations are implemented with scrollmagic. The page was built to load fast (score of x on pagespeed) and to be presentable on Google and Facebook links.
The only thing that stayed the same, compared to the previous version, was the logo. The new design kept the colors and was built around the idea of light design, which is the central occupation of the person presented.

ASKARI ZEREN

AskariZeren is a german-based real estate boutique that needed a new page to present their products which are not only real estates to sell, but also consulting for vendors and buyers.

MADE WITH

The goal was to present the different areas of expertise in a clear and concise way while conveying a reputable look and feel. The backend needed to be manageable, especially for adding and removing real estate objects.

RESULTS

The page was built with Boostrap 3, based on the Agency template. To highlight the detailed content of the different areas, small subpages were created that don't require the page to reload completeley.
The template was implemented with wordpress with help from the ACF-Plugin. Multiple options for representing and managing the real estates were considered and an iFrame has been prepared and will soon be implemented to replace the current static content.
