BRAGI

Bragi is a leading innovater in Hearable Computing and brought the first wireless smart earphone to the market, long before Apple decided to ditch the headphone jack.
As fast moving company, they required up-to-date presentation of ther products. To mirror their approach, their marketing pages also needed to be modern and innovative.
The goal was to implement pages for the new products based on the designer's template, as well as to migrate existing pages from multiple platforms to WordPress.

MADE WITH

The challenge was to build Marketing Pages that would run smoothly with custom animations and background videos. As multiple developers were collaborating on the project, a workflow was developed, that allowed for synchronized merging on local, testing, and live environments. Some of the existing websites had been build with different systems and partially lacked a backend. To ensure optimal manageability, they were be migrated to one CMS.

RESULTS

The pages are built with the Bootstrap Framework and most of the animations are implemented with ScrollMagic. The local develoment environment was set up with Vagrant and synchronized with Github. To improve the performance, the content is delivered by a CDN from Amazon Web Services and Google Analytics was implemented to measure user interaction on the Marketing Pages as well as on the shop.
The migration of the sites to Wordpress required the CMS to have a customized template, compatibility with a CDN and the ability to add language options, which was achieved by multiple plugins and custom solutions.

VERTEMO

Vertemo is an Event Technician's website serving mainly as a business card. The original page was outdated, so a complete redesign was necessary.

MADE WITH

Since the page has mainly representative purpose, it needed to be simple, clear and concise. The design was based on the one page template Agency. The content is not expected to change often and the web-space has limited capacity and functionality, so the page is pure Front-End without CMS.

RESULTS

The template is based on Bootstrap 3 and the scrolling animations are implemented with scrollmagic. The page was built to load fast and to be presentable on Google and Facebook links.
The only remnants are the logo and the color-scheme and the new design is built around light effects, which also are the company’s central occupation.

ASKARI ZEREN

AskariZeren is a German real estate boutique that needed a new page to present their products which are not only real estates for sale, but also consulting for vendors and buyers.

MADE WITH

The goal was to present the different areas of expertise in a clear and concise way, while conveying a reputable look and feel. The backend needed to be manageable, especially for adding and removing real estate objects.

RESULTS

The page was built with Boostrap 3, based on the Agency template. To highlight the detailed content of the different areas, small subpages were created that don't require the page to reload completeley.
The template was implemented with WordPress with help from the ACF-Plugin. Multiple options for representing and managing the real estates were considered and an iFrame has been prepared and will soon be implemented to replace the current static content.
